SmartDraft - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 29, 2025

1. Information We Collect

SmartDraft collects minimal information necessary for functionality:

  • User Profile Data: Your name and professional background (stored locally)
  • API Key: Your OpenAI API key (stored securely in Chrome sync storage)
  • Email Drafts: Generated email drafts (stored locally before insertion)
  • Usage Statistics: Basic usage counts (emails generated, last used date)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Generate personalized email content using AI
  • Provide usage statistics within the extension
  • Improve email generation quality based on your profile

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: All data is stored locally on your device
  • Chrome Sync: Only API key and profile sync across your Chrome browsers
  • No External Servers: We don't store any data on external servers
  • Encryption: Chrome handles encryption for sync storage

4. Third-Party Services

SmartDraft integrates with:

  • OpenAI API: For email generation (subject to OpenAI's privacy policy)
  • Gmail: For email insertion (no data sent to Google beyond normal Gmail usage)

5. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer your personal information to third parties, except:

  • Email content sent to OpenAI API for generation (as required for functionality)
  • When required by law or to protect our rights

6. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your stored data through the extension settings
  • Delete your data by uninstalling the extension
  • Modify your profile information at any time
  • Clear local storage through Chrome settings

7. Data Retention

  • Data is retained until you uninstall the extension
  • You can manually clear data through extension settings
  • Chrome sync data follows Google's retention policies

8. Children's Privacy

SmartDraft is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy. Changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.

10. Contact Information

For privacy-related questions, please contact us through the Chrome Web Store developer contact information.

11. Compliance

This extension complies with:

  • Chrome Web Store Developer Program Policies
  • Google's User Data Policy
  • General data protection best practices
